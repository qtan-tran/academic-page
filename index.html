Open Science Researcher | Postdoc, Bielefeld University

📍 Hamburg & Bielefeld, Germany

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Bielefeld University working at the intersection of open infrastructure, the sociology of knowledge, and the philosophy of science. My work examines how scholarly communication systems shape epistemic communities, and how open, community-governed infrastructure can support more equitable knowledge production.

Beyond research, I am interested in AI/ML agents and their role in academic publishing workflows, as well as literature and literary criticism as lenses for understanding knowledge practices.
